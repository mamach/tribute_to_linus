Here is a brief timeline of Linus Torvalds...

  • Born in Helsinki, Finland in 1969.
  • Attended University of Helsinki between 1988 and 1996.
  • Graduated in Master in Computer Scince from NODES research group.
  • M.Sc Thesis : Linux- A portable Operating System
  • Joined Finnish army in 1989.
  • 1990 - Exposed to the UNIX for the first time.
  • 1991- First Prototype of Linux released
  • 1994 - Version1.0 of Linux released
  • 1997-2003 -- worked in company called Transmeta

That's what makes Linux so good: you put in something, and that effort multiplies. It's a positive feedback cycle.

-- Linus Torvalds
